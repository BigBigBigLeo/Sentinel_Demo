3. Sentinel unifies risk, execution, and accountability in one operating system, turning uncertain farming into evidence-based growth.

5. We load a real scenario and push each step forward, with a traceable record for every action.
6. Investors see a repeatable operating model, and clients get reliable on-ground execution.
7. Sensor, pest, environmental, and image data are continuously ingested and cross-validated in real time.

10. Iterative reasoning step one: detect anomalies and locate impact zones.
11. Step two: aggregate multimodal evidence and validate that the anomaly is not random noise.

14. Step five: enforce PHI, restricted-chemical rules, wind limits, and buffer-zone constraints.
15. The output is a structured prescription with fallback paths that is executable and auditable.

17. Agronomy, watchdog, compliance, execution, audit, and finance agents run in parallel with clear roles.

20. Sentinel compares prescribed actions against actual execution in real time and writes an execution fingerprint.
